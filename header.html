About the Ultima Ratio Project

The Ultima Ratio Project aims to develop a centralised database of historical muzzle-loading artillery guns. Launched in June 2018, the Project seeks to engage with scholars, curators, arms specialists, historical preservation organisations, individual researchers, and other key stakeholders in recording the history of important muzzle-loading ‘big guns’, and making such data publicly available.
